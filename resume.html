Summary

Nine years in conversational AI and language AI, the last six and a half designing and building conversation AI through three generations of the technology: NLU assistants, prompt-engineered LLM experiences, and agentic systems. I define how AI behaves (what it says, what it does, and where it stops) and prove it's good enough to ship: prompt engineering, golden datasets, LLM-as-judge and human-in-the-loop evaluation. A design technologist who ships: most recently a working agent skill built solo in five days, with its own 108-case eval dataset. Led the work and the people doing it as a manager of three.
