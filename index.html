About

I'm a staff content designer and model UX writer who thinks in systems. Language isn't the frosting on an AI product — it's part of the system. I write the words customers hear and see, and I define the behavior behind them: what the model should ask, retrieve, refuse, summarize, and hand off, and how anyone can tell whether the response was good enough to ship.

That through-line runs the length of my career. I trained language models at Meta, taught Siri to speak at Apple, owned retail support assistants at Sam's Club, engineered shipped consumer LLM products at Credit Karma, and now lead AI response-quality evaluation for QuickBooks' agentic assistant. Different companies, one job: closing the gap between what a product can do and what it actually says to a person.
