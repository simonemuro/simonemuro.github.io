About

I work in the space where product intent becomes model behavior.

I design AI agents that know when to act, ask, stop, and hand off. Then I build the prototype, write the evals, and turn product decisions into model behavior teams can test.

I started in language-model training and NLU, moved through production voice and chat assistants, then into LLM and agentic systems. My edge is translating messy customer problems into behaviors an AI system can execute, evaluate, and safely hand off.

Selected work

Six cases, from LLM classifiers to agent skills.

Billing skill · agentic era

Built the agent skill that explains QuickBooks subscription charges.

A solo build in a five-day internal hackathon, on years of billing research: account-aware answers, a cancel-intercept widget, and the 108-case eval it passed. Leadership buy-in at 80% built.

Cancellation saves · LLM era

Designed a saves flow and LLM classifier for cancellation intent.

An LLM classifier reading why customers cancel, routing truly savable reasons to a specialist, and giving everyone else a clean goodbye. It ran end to end in 2023; both routes are on the page as screen recordings.

Voice · LLM era

Designed the assistant’s spoken answers on the QuickBooks phone line.

Voice has no screen to rescue it, so the work was deciding what the model could say in seconds, when to clarify, and when to hand off. Shipped, with a measured +3.9-point containment lift, then full production.

Credit Karma · LLM era, 2024

The model knows your approval odds. It’s never allowed to say them.

One workbook, eleven revisions, nine synthetic members to grade against, and a rubric where a single legal or safety miss fails the run. It’s all on the page.

Payroll tax skill · agentic era

Got the payroll tax diagnosis out of experts’ heads and into Intuit Intelligence.

Golden guidance for every priority payroll question in about ten days, a generator that drafted diagnostic reasoning jurisdiction by jurisdiction, and the UAT that graded the live skills against it.

Quality program · agentic era

Every agent answer is graded against an answer key. I built the practice that writes it.

Generator prompts do the first draft, twelve QA checks screen every batch, and experts keep the final say. Turnaround from about three weeks to about one.
