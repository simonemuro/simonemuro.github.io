About

I’m a Staff Content Designer and Model UX Writer who thinks in systems. My background blends UX writing, conversation design, prompt and skill design, technical communication, product strategy, and AI evaluation infrastructure. I got into this work because language is not the frosting on an AI product. It is part of the system itself.

I write the content customers hear and see, but I also define the behavior behind it: what the model should ask, retrieve, refuse, summarize, hand off, and how we know whether the response was good enough to ship.
