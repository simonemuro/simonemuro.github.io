Three generations of the technology

Every era taught the same lesson: start with the problem, not the technology.

I start with the customer's problem, then choose the technology that actually solves it. The tools changed three times. That never did.

2016 – 2022 · NLU

Intent by intent

The problem was being understood. Content-policy models, speech for Siri, then full ownership of voice and chat assistants: intents, training, tuning, and the analytics that told the truth.
