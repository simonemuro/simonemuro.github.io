About

I work in the space where product intent becomes model behavior.

I design AI agents that know when to act, ask, stop, and hand off. Then I build the prototype, write the evals, and turn product decisions into model behavior teams can test.

I started in language-model training and NLU, moved through production voice and chat assistants, then into LLM and agentic systems. My edge is translating messy customer problems into behaviors an AI system can execute, evaluate, and safely hand off.

Classifier strategy · responsible AI
