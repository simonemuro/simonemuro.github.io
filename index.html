About

I started in language-model training and NLU, moved through Siri and production voice/chat assistants, then into LLM and agentic systems at Intuit. My edge is translating messy customer problems into behaviors an AI system can execute, evaluate, and safely hand off.

I am comfortable as the product lead, the conversation designer, the eval owner, and the builder who opens the repo when the artifact needs to exist.

How I work

The operating system is simple.

  1. 01Define the job. What outcome should this agent own, and what belongs to a human?
  2. 02Write the behavior. Prompts, tools, states, failure paths, and handoff rules.
  3. 03Build the proof. Prototypes, mock tools, scenario injection, and golden data.
  4. 04Gate the launch. LLM judges, human review, regressions, and clear stop conditions.
