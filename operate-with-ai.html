01One call

The call that set the rule.

This build made hundreds of calls. One of them decided what every number on this site is allowed to say. The claim could be kept on memory, confirmed and kept, or deleted outright. Her call: repair.

Decision 08: the claim with no receipt

A number on the old site had no receipt. Memory said it was right. Four archives said nothing.

A row in the claims census
The setup

Before this build wrote a page, every number on the old site and the resumes behind it went into a census of her own archives, four sources: local files, screenshots, email, decks. Most claims came back holding a receipt. This one came back holding nothing, and memory still said it was true.

repair.

Rewrite it down to what the receipts support. Where nothing supports it, it comes off the page.

The receipt

The fix became a rule, and the rule became a ledger: every number on this site traces to an artifact she owns, found in the four-source census. Claims that failed were corrected or removed, here and in the 38 resume files that carried them. A portfolio that argues for eval discipline does not get to grade itself on a curve.

02The build

A cold read, a competition, then a fleet.

The brief came from strangers, the design from a contest, the pages from agent crews. Every step is in the trace below.

The cold read

Eight persona judges read the old site first, no context, no charity: hiring managers, a recruiter, a skeptical engineer, an eval practitioner, critics. Where they agreed, the gap became a requirement.

The competition

Design direction was settled by competition, not debate: three agents each built a full homepage, and a judge panel read them cold. The light editorial system won; the runtime trace and the working refusal came over from the losers.

The crews

One agent built the shared system: type, tokens, components, budgets. Then a fleet built the pages in parallel, one agent per page, this page included. The direction and the final say stayed with her.

The gates

Every page faced three judges before merge: redaction, honesty, voice. A page that failed a gate went back with notes. No pass, no ship.

03The method

Direction, judgment, evidence. The typing is delegated.

Every claim is gated before it ships. It is the same shape as the day job: define what good looks like, build it, prove it.

Feedback, two sizes

Minor meant tighten this and keep going. Major meant stop, re-plan, and show the new plan before the next line. Naming which one you are giving is most of the craft of directing agents.

What I did not build

A live model on this site. A site agent that bluffs would falsify every case it hosts, so the box on the home page answers from curated replies, refuses the rest, and says so in its own header.
